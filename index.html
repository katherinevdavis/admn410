History: The Durham House of Pets has been around since 2008. It was founded by Katherine Davis, who had always been a pet lover and wanted a shop where she could get all she needed for her pets close to home. Katherine lives in Durham and thought this would be the greatest investment she could have ever done. Since 2008, the store has grown. Starting with just treats and food, to now toys, pet apparel and various necessities that any animal would need.

Why Use Us: Durham House of Pets is the perfect location, located in the heart of downtown Durham. Pets are always welcome, as long as they are on leashes! Our store has all the necessities for any dog, cat or fish. Our employees are also very open to help and take care of the pets while you shop for your furry and fishy friends!

Policies

Financing: We take all credit cards; Mastercard, Visa and Discover. We also take cash.

Delivery: We do ship all of our stores items found on this webpage. It should take between 3-7 business days based on where you are located. We only deliver within the Northeast Region. Shipping costs are $4.95.
